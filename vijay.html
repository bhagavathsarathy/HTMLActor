Vijay, born Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar on June 22, 1974, is a prominent Indian actor, playback singer, and politician who primarily works in Tamil cinema. He is one of the most popular and highest-paid actors in India, with a significant fan following often referring to him as "Thalapathy," meaning "commander" in Tamil. Born in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, Vijay is the son of film director S. A. Chandrasekhar and playback singer Shoba Chandrasekhar. He studied at Balalok Matriculation Higher Secondary School and later attended Loyola College, Chennai, though he dropped out to focus on his acting career.
